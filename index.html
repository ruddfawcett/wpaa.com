Reserving WPAA Equipment

If you would like WPAA to provide equipment for an event please email Scott MacDonald (smacdonald@andover.edu) Please contact us at the start of the term in which the event will occur, or a minimum of 2 weeks in advance. If you would like a WPAA DJ to play music at an event, please contact us at least 3 weeks in advance. We cannot guarantee the availability of any services.

Recording

WPAA offers recording to Phillips Academy students. If you are a musician or vocalist, we can help record, edit and produce a digital recording. Our recording studio offers professional quality recording and we can record anything from a single musician to a band. Keep an eye out for the occasional WPAA Recording Sessions, where walk-ins can make quick recordings. If you are looking for a specialized recording session, please contact the Head of Recording to schedule an appointment.
